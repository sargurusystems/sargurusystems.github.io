Our Story

Trusted IT Service Partner
Since 2015

Sarguru Systems was founded with a simple mission: to provide honest, affordable and high-quality IT services to the people of Tiruchengode and the surrounding regions.

With over 8 years in the industry, we have grown to become a trusted name for laptop, desktop and Apple Mac repairs across Chennai, Tiruchengode, Erode, Namakkal, Salem and Karur. Our positive customer feedback and repeat business drive our relentless commitment to excellence.

Our team of highly skilled technicians is proficient in diagnosing and servicing all major brands — delivering fast, dependable solutions at prices that make professional IT care accessible to everyone.

  • Expert chip-level repair for all laptop and desktop brands
  • Certified CCTV installation and security system setup
  • Comprehensive networking and structured cabling solutions
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Genuine spare parts with service warranty
  • AMC contracts for offices, shops and businesses
